Mediterranean Quinoa Salad

A healthy, vibrant, and hearty salad packed with fluffy quinoa, briny feta, olives, and fresh Mediterranean vegetables.

Prep: 15 min | Cook: 15 min
Servings: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Quinoa (rinsed well) 1 cup
  • Chicken or Vegetable Broth 2 cups
  • English Cucumber (diced) 1
  • Cherry Tomatoes (halved) 1 pint
  • Kalamata Olives (pitted, sliced) 1/2 cup
  • Feta Cheese (crumbled) 1/2 cup
  • Fresh Parsley (chopped) 1/4 cup

Lemon-Herb Dressing

  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil 1/4 cup
  • Fresh Lemon Juice 2 tbsp
  • Dried Oregano 1 tsp

Instructions

  1. Cook Quinoa: Combine the rinsed quinoa and 2 cups of broth in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes, or until all liquid is absorbed. Remove from heat, fluff with a fork, and let it cool completely.

  2. Make Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper until well combined. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.

  3. Chop Vegetables: While the quinoa cools, dice the cucumber, halve the cherry tomatoes, slice the olives, and chop the fresh parsley.

  4. Combine Salad: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooled quinoa, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, olives, red onion, and fresh parsley.

  5. Dress and Serve: Pour the lemon-herb dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat. Finally, fold in the crumbled feta cheese. Serve immediately or chill for better flavor absorption.

Nutritional Facts (per serving)

Calories 340 kcal
Protein 12 g
Fat 18 g
Carbs 35 g

Approximate values based on ingredients listed.

Chef's Notes & Tips

**Rinsing the quinoa** is essential to remove the naturally occurring saponin coating, which can taste bitter or soapy. For a richer flavor, use chicken or vegetable broth instead of water when cooking the quinoa.
