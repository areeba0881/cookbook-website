Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Quick and indulgent pasta tossed in a rich, velvety sauce made with fresh garlic and plenty of Parmesan cheese.

Prep: 10 min | Cook: 15 min
Servings: 4
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine or Spaghetti 1 lb
  • Butter 2 tbsp
  • Garlic (minced) 4 cloves
  • Heavy Cream 1 1/2 cups
  • Parmesan Cheese (freshly grated) 1 cup
  • Chicken or Vegetable Broth 1/2 cup
  • Fresh Parsley (chopped) 2 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Cook Pasta: Cook the pasta according to package directions until al dente. Reserve about 1 cup of the starchy pasta water before draining. Set the pasta aside.

  2. Sauté Garlic: In a large, deep skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. **Do not let the garlic brown.**

  3. Make the Sauce: Pour in the heavy cream and chicken broth (or reserved pasta water). Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, then reduce the heat to low and let it cook for 5 minutes, allowing it to thicken slightly.

  4. Add Cheese: Remove the skillet from the heat. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is completely smooth and creamy. Season heavily with salt and pepper.

  5. Combine: Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the sauce. Toss everything together until the pasta is fully coated. If the sauce is too thick, add a little of the reserved pasta water until it reaches your desired consistency. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley.

Nutritional Facts (per serving)

Calories 650 kcal
Protein 25 g
Fat 40 g
Carbs 50 g

Approximate values based on ingredients listed.

Chef's Notes & Tips

For an extra luxurious sauce, use half-and-half instead of broth, or add a pinch of nutmeg when simmering the cream. Always grate your Parmesan fresh—pre-grated cheese often contains anti-caking agents that make the sauce grainy.
