Truffle Mushroom Pizza

A luxurious white pizza topped with sautéed wild mushrooms, creamy cheese, and aromatic truffle oil.

Prep: 15 min | Cook: 15 min
Servings: 2-3
Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough 1 ball
  • Ricotta Cheese 1/2 cup
  • Shredded Mozzarella 1 cup
  • Assorted Mushrooms (cremini, shiitake) 8 oz
  • Garlic (minced) 2 cloves
  • Truffle Oil (for drizzling) 2 tbsp
  • Fresh Thyme 1 tsp

Instructions

  1. Prep Dough & Oven: Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). Roll out the pizza dough onto a baking sheet or pizza stone. Lightly brush the crust edges with olive oil.

  2. Sauté Mushrooms: In a skillet, sauté the sliced mushrooms and minced garlic in a drizzle of olive oil until they are softened and the moisture has evaporated (about 5-7 minutes). Season with salt, pepper, and fresh thyme.

  3. Assemble Pizza: Spread the ricotta cheese evenly over the pizza dough, leaving a 1-inch border for the crust. Sprinkle the mozzarella on top. Distribute the sautéed mushrooms evenly over the cheese.

  4. Bake: Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbling slightly.

  5. Finish & Serve: Carefully remove the pizza from the oven. Drizzle generously with truffle oil before slicing into wedges and serving immediately.

Nutritional Facts (per serving)

Calories 550 kcal
Protein 30 g
Fat 35 g
Carbs 45 g

Approximate values based on ingredients listed.

Chef's Notes & Tips

For a richer, deeper flavor, use a blend of Parmesan and Pecorino Romano cheese with the mozzarella. You can also roast the mushrooms ahead of time to intensify their flavor.
